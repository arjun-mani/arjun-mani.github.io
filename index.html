Research

Point and Ask: Incorporating Pointing into Visual Question Answering

Arjun Mani, Will Hinthorn, Nobline Yoo, Olga Russakovsky

ArXiv Project page

Extends Visual Question Answering (VQA) to questions requiring pointing gestures, a key aspect of human communication about the visual world. (1) Defines three types of questions in this space with unique reasoning challenges, and (2) introduces benchmark datasets and baseline models for each question type.
